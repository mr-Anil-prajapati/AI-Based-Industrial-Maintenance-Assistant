Compressor Troubleshooting Guide

High compressor temperature may be caused by:
- Blocked cooling air path
- Dirty cooler fins
- High ambient temperature
- Excessive discharge pressure
- Lubrication problems

Recommended checks:
1. Inspect fan operation and airflow.
2. Clean heat exchanger surfaces.
3. Review suction and discharge pressure trends.
4. Check oil level and maintenance schedule.

Safety reference:
- ISO 12100 hazard reduction requires manual verification before maintenance intervention.
