PLC Alarm Table

MTR01_INTERLOCK:
Description: Motor-1 safety interlock open.
Expected action: Inspect guard switch chain, overload auxiliary contact, and start permissives.

CMP01_HIGH_TEMP:
Description: Compressor-1 temperature above high threshold.
Expected action: Inspect cooling system, filters, airflow, and pressure conditions.

CV01_OVERLOAD:
Description: Conveyor-1 overload trip.
Expected action: Check for mechanical binding, excess material load, and motor current.

CV01_JAM:
Description: Conveyor-1 jam detected.
Expected action: Inspect transfer zone and remove blockage under approved safety procedure.
