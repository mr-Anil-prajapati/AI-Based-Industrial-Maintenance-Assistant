# Pump Maintenance Notes

Typical pump failure checks:

- suction valve position
- cavitation indicators
- motor overload condition
- seal leakage
- discharge blockage

Recommended diagnostic sequence:

1. Compare start command, run feedback, and permissive inputs.
2. Review alarm bits and analog trends for pressure and temperature.
3. Inspect suction conditions and downstream restriction.
4. Record actions under plant quality and safety procedure.
